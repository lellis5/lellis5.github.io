#4: Ghost of Tsushima

Buy here

I like this game a lot and I had 10 hours on it after having it for only a few days. The combat, story, and graphics are all amazing and the only reason it isnt higher is because I havent finished it yet.

Buy here

This is in my opinion the best tower defense game of all time. The gameplay doesnt get repetetive, the story is funny, the monkeys have interesting synergies and there are so many different upgrades between them all. The bloons series makes consistantky good games
