This stage can be called the architect stage because in it you're mapping out your website and how everything will fit together. Make sure your website has a clear structure so its easy for your client to navigate through the various pages. You should start with a site map. A site map is where you lay out all of the pages that need to exist and think about how to format them. You also need a content index where you plan all the information that needs to go on each page and why its needed
